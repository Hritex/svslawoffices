The Firm

A practice built on preparation, judgment and trust.

SVS Law Offices is a full-service law firm headquartered in New Delhi, representing clients all across India.

The firm appears before the District Courts, the High Court of Delhi, High Courts across the country, tribunals and regulatory authorities, and the Supreme Court of India. It advises a diverse base of clients, from individuals and family enterprises to companies and institutional bodies, on both contentious and advisory work. Each engagement is led personally by a partner, ensuring that the strategy a client is promised is the strategy a client receives.

Our approach is deliberately unhurried at the desk and decisive in the courtroom: matters are researched thoroughly, risks are mapped honestly, and clients are counselled on the path that actually serves their interest, not merely the one that prolongs a file.

What We Do

Practice areas

Focused expertise across the matters most often faced by individuals and businesses operating in Delhi and across India.

01

Dispute Resolution & Civil Litigation

Conduct of civil and commercial suits from institution to execution, recovery proceedings, specific performance, declaratory and partition suits, injunctions and interim relief, revisions, first and second appeals, writ petitions and special leave petitions. The firm appears before District Courts, Commercial Courts, the High Court of Delhi, High Courts across India and the Supreme Court.

02

Criminal Law

Defence and prosecution across the criminal spectrum under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita and the Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, anticipatory and regular bail, quashing petitions, discharge, trial, revision and appeal. Advisory and representation in economic offences, cheque dishonour under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, cheating and forgery, offences under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, and proceedings before investigating agencies.

03

Arbitration & Alternative Dispute Resolution

Drafting and vetting of arbitration agreements; appointment of arbitrators under Section 11; interim protection under Section 9; conduct of domestic and institutional arbitrations; and challenge, setting aside and enforcement of awards under Sections 34 and 36 of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The firm also advises on mediation and conciliation under the Mediation Act, 2023.

04

Electricity & Energy Laws

Advisory and representation under the Electricity Act, 2003, tariff determination and truing-up, change-in-law compensation, power purchase and transmission agreements, open access, wheeling and cross-subsidy surcharge, licensing and renewable energy obligations. Appearances before the Central and State Electricity Regulatory Commissions, the Appellate Tribunal for Electricity and the Supreme Court.

05

Media, Entertainment & Technology Laws

Defamation actions and defence, content takedown and intermediary liability under the Information Technology Act and the IT Rules, 2021, pre-publication and pre-broadcast review, personality and publicity rights, copyright and trademark protection, film and music production, licensing and distribution agreements, advertising standards, and data protection compliance under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

06

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

Drafting and negotiation of commercial contracts, shareholder and joint venture agreements; company incorporation, secretarial and statutory compliance under the Companies Act, 2013; legal and title due diligence; mergers, acquisitions and restructuring; insolvency proceedings before the NCLT and NCLAT under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code; and day-to-day advisory for promoters, start-ups and established enterprises.

07

Real Estate & Property

Title investigation and search reports, conveyancing, sale deeds, gift and relinquishment deeds, lease, licence and development agreements, collaboration and builder-buyer arrangements. Representation in RERA proceedings, landlord-tenant and eviction matters, specific performance suits, and disputes concerning possession, easements and encroachment.

08

Taxation & Regulatory

Advisory and litigation on direct tax, including assessment, reassessment, penalty and appeals before the Commissioner (Appeals), the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal and the High Court. Indirect tax and GST advisory, classification and input credit disputes, show-cause notices, adjudication and appellate proceedings; and representation before regulatory and quasi-judicial authorities.

09

Family, Matrimonial & Succession

Divorce by mutual consent and contested divorce, maintenance under Section 125 BNSS and the Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, custody and guardianship, restitution of conjugal rights, and proceedings under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act. Drafting of wills, trusts, gift and family settlement deeds; probate, letters of administration and succession certificates; and inheritance and partition disputes, handled with sensitivity and discretion.
